In 1999 - The First International Gathering of Motorcycle Tourists.



Also known as "GOBLIN SHOW", was held in Odessa On the territory of the Burevestnik camp, district 411 Batteries. Andrey "Goblin" became the organizer and ideological inspirer. The whole event took place over 2 days. The number of participants then did not exceed 60 motorcyclists from Ukraine and Moldova.



In 2000 and 2001 the same organizer held the 2nd and 3rd, respectively, International Gatherings of motorcycle tourists "GOBLIN SHOW", in Odessa, on the territory of the "Pavlov's House", Luzanovka district. The geography of these gatherings was already much wider. More than 200 participants came from all over Ukraine, Russia, Belarus, Moldova and Poland. The events lasted 2 days and included a parade of an organized motorcycle column through the central streets of the city and rock concerts with the participation of groups from Odessa



In 2002, the 4th International Gathering of Motorcycle Tourists “GOBLIN SHOW” was also held in Odessa on the territory of the Odeskabel OJSC stadium, total. sq. 15000 sq.m. More than 400 participants arrived from England, Sweden, Germany, Austria, Poland, Russia, Belarus, Moldova and Ukraine. The rally took place over 2 days, during which a parade of moto-nations was organized around the city and a rock festival was held on its own professional stage with rented concert lighting equipment and sound with a power of 25 kW. Groups from Moscow, Chisinau, Odessa performed at the festival. The event was attended by over 1000 citizens.



In 2003, the 5th International Gathering of Motorcycle Tourists “GOBLIN SHOW” was held at the same place, the Odeskabel stadium, and has already gathered more than 600 motorcyclists from the CIS countries and Europe. The event lasted 3 days. As part of the rally, the First Moto-Rock Music Festival was held, at which more than 15 rock bands from Russia and Ukraine performed. This time, the rally property was significantly increased: pavilions with a total area of 360 sq.m. were added to the professional stage 12x7x8 m. and furniture for 700 seats. For 3 days, the rally was attended by more than 2 thousand citizens.



In 2004, the 6th International gathering of motorcycle tourists "GOBLIN SHOW" was held in the same place, at the Odeskabel stadium. The number of participants has grown to 800 motorcyclists from Europe and the CIS countries. The event took place over 7 days. Both individual excursions to the sights of the city of Odessa, and a general sightseeing motorcycle walk in the form of a moto parade for the participants of the rally were organized. The motor-column parade passed through the central streets of the city, including along the street. Deribasovskaya! As part of the gathering, a 4-day rock festival "Moto-Rock-2" has already been held. More than 20 groups performed at the festival, which included both young and quite popular bands from the CIS countries. In addition to the property of the rally, a professional boxing ring was purchased, where demonstration fights of the city's athletes were held with the support of the sports club "Captain". The rally was attended by more than 3 thousand citizens.



In 2005, the 7th International Motorcycle Rally "GOBLIN SHOW" was held on the territory of the permanent partner of the rally - the stadium of JSC "Odeskabel". More than 1000 participants from Norway, Sweden, Holland, England, Denmark, Germany, Austria, Hungary, Poland, Russia, Belarus, Moldova and Ukraine came to the event, which has long become popular and well-known outside of Ukraine. Even motorcyclists from the city of Nakhodka came to the rally, having overcome a path of 14 thousand km. Deribasovskaya with an hourly parking of a motorcycle column organized on it, where Odessans and guests of the city had the opportunity to get to know the two-wheeled vehicles and their owners. More than 20 bands from Russia, Belarus, Moldova and Ukraine took part in the 4-day Moto-Rock-3 music festival. A 2-day championship in "Biker Fights Without Rules" and demonstration fights of athletes were added to the constant entertainment program of the rally. The 7-day event was attended by over 5,000 people. a person interested in moto technology and rock music. All meetings were held with the support of the City Hall, the Ministry of Internal Affairs and the traffic police of Odessa.

Achievements of 2005: Advantages of the rally The rally takes place on the territory within the city, on the only central road, which allows guests to freely move around the city in order to get acquainted with the sights (Deribasovskaya, Arcadia, catacombs, the Opera House, nightclubs, bars, restaurants) and easily get to the drop off point. The territory of the rally is located in close proximity to the sea (Luzanovka beach), where along the embankment there are many discos, nightclubs, bars, restaurants. Gathering guests can have a great time on the beach! The venue itself is a stadium 130*120m, which allows you to place a stage, pavilions, tables, a ring, etc. Everything is in one place and this ensures unhindered viewing of show programs, even sitting at a table and talking with friends. Accommodation is located in close proximity to the stadium on the seashore. There are both inexpensive rooms at the rate of $ 5 per person (3-4 people), and suites with a toilet, shower, TV, refrigerator, European-quality repair, at a very affordable price - $ 15 per person (2 people). Meals are organized both on the territory of the rally and in neighboring bars on the beach. Prices on the territory of the rally are the most democratic in the entire CIS. We meet guests for a whole week, which allows you to perfectly get to know the city, the beach, the sea, the girls, and when it all gets boring and 100% of the participants arrive by Thursday, you can enjoy 4x. afternoon rock festival, contests, fights, balloons, beer, socializing. A pleasant tradition for the male part of the rally has become a free entrance for girls, which, by the way, is the first time in Ukraine and the CIS!!!



For the first time in Ukraine, registration fees and registration paraphernalia were introduced: T-shirts, stickers and quality badges unique in Ukraine. The rally parade passes through the central streets of the city with an hour-long stop in the very heart of Odessa - st. Deribasovskaya (pedestrian zone). The rally parade is the second, after Yumorina, an event that is allowed to pass along the street. Deribasovskaya. The rally was recognized as the "Best Motorcycle Rally 2005 in the CIS" by the 4th International Conference on Motorcycle Tourism, held in December 2005. , in Moscow and was awarded a certificate of honor and a valuable prize.
